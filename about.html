A New Soup

In the heart of every home, lies an ancient tradition that has survived the test of time. Early on, mankind harnessed fire - the crucial element to extract the decadent flavors from the nourishment they hunted and gathered. Through time, humans developed a method of domesticating the game they onced hunted and plotted fields to centralize the plants they once gathered. From every end of the world, humans began throwing the ingredients they possessed into pots crafted by hand. Fires bursting underneath the elegant vessels caressing the food, a savory aroma conquered the air - invading the nostrils lingering nearby. The new innovation, soup, would nourish and sustain many.
