Welcome To Homies!

We are a platform that advocates for the homeless - we are passionate about sharing the individual stories in each of your communities. Our goal is to make it easier for you to make informed donations and provide resources to those in need.

How We Help!

We form partnerships with local food kitchens, homeless shelters, and homeless communities to provide a deeper understanding of why homelessness exists. We provide an easy to use platform for donors to inform themselves and then take action. Each donor can select how their donation will be used!
